Meet Squeege the Bottlenose Dolphin

Squeege is a friendly and playful bottlenose dolphin who loves to swim and explore the ocean. He is known for his high-energy acrobatic displays and his ability to communicate with his human friends.

Fun Facts about Squeege

  • Squeege was born in the warm waters of the Caribbean Sea.
  • He is an expert hunter and can locate prey using echolocation.
  • Squeege is a skilled vocal learner and can imitate many different sounds.
  • He loves to play with toys and interact with people in a supervised setting.
